About Me

Welcome to my page! I'm Joanna, a master's student in neurotechnology at Imperial College London. I'm currently working on modeling neurotransmitters in neural organoids based on electrochemistry. We're characterizing how serotonin signalling works in human neurons and how it's impacted by pharmaceuticals like SSRIs - all to understand and develop better depression treatments.

During my studies, I've focused biosignal processing, computational modeling and bioinstrumentation design. I recently graduated from Universtity of California San Diego where I built EEG processing and analysis pipelines for neuropsychiatric biomarker development. In general, I'm motivated by the idea of precision psychiatry - knowing someone's neurobiology, can we make them their perfect treatment? I think so.
